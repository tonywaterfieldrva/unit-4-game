Crystal Collector Game


Instructions

A random number between 19 and 120 will be chosen and that is the Target Points you are attempting to match.

The four crytals displayed are assigned a points number between 1 and 12

Each click of a crystal will add to the Gem Points.

You Win if you Match the Target Points, you Lose if you exceed the Target Points.

A Victory or Buzzer sound will play and the Win/Loss tally will update. The game will reset and begin a new game.
